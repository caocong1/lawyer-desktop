California Trade Secret Precedents (CUTSA)

1. Altavion, Inc. v. Konica Minolta Systems Laboratory, Inc. (2014)

Clarified that "ideas" can be protected under CUTSA if they possess independent economic value and are subject to reasonable secrecy efforts.

2. Silvaco Data Systems v. Intel Corp. (2010)

Established that trade secret misappropriation must be based on CUTSA; common law claims are preempted if they rely on the same nucleus of facts.
